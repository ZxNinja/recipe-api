Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the Plate Plan mobile application or service.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of personal data necessary to allow you to use our services and features.

Information Collected via Facebook Login:

When you choose to log in to Plate Plan using your Facebook account, you grant us permission to access the following data from your Facebook profile:

Data Type Purpose of Collection
Name To personalize your experience within the app (e.g., greeting you by name).
Email Address To communicate with you regarding your account, updates, or support issues.
Facebook User ID To uniquely identify your account in our system.

No Other Information Collected:

We do not collect any other personal information from your device (such as location, photos, contacts, or calendar data) unless we explicitly ask you for permission and you grant it. We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect your data outside of the Facebook Login process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To allow you to securely log into and access your account in the app.
  • Account Management: To manage and maintain your account and provide customer support.
  • Communication: To send you non-marketing, transactional emails (e.g., password reset, support replies).
  • Service Functionality: To provide the core functionality of the Plate Plan service.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We only share your information with third parties in the following limited circumstances:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook): Your data is initially provided to us by Meta/Facebook, and our use of this data is subject to the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by a public authority (e.g., a court order or government agency).

4. Data Retention and Security

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, and unauthorized use or modification.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal data.

To request data deletion:

If you wish to remove your account and have the data we collected through Facebook Login deleted from our systems, you can do so by:

  • Contacting us: Send an email to plateplan@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and your Facebook User ID or registered email address.
  • Via Facebook: You can remove the Plate Plan app from your list of authorized applications in your Facebook Settings. When you do this, Meta will send us a notification, and we will delete your data within 30 days.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
