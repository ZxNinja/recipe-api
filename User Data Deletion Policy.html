Plate Plan User Data Deletion Policy

This policy details the process for users to request the deletion of their personal data collected by the Plate Plan mobile application, primarily through **Facebook Login**.

Your Right to Deletion

As outlined in our main Privacy Policy, you have the right to request the deletion of all personal data we have collected about you. This typically includes your **Name**, **Email Address**, **Contact/Phone Number**, and **Facebook User ID**.

Once your data is deleted, your access to the Plate Plan service will be permanently revoked, and you will need to create a new account to use the service again.

Methods for Data Deletion

You have two simple ways to initiate the data deletion process:

  1. Via Facebook Settings (Recommended):
    • Go to the **"Apps and Websites"** section within your Facebook Settings.
    • Find and select the **"Plate Plan"** application.
    • Click the **"Remove"** button to revoke Plate Plan's access to your data.
    • What Happens Next: When you remove the app from your Facebook settings, Meta sends us an automated notification. Upon receiving this notification, we will delete all associated data from our servers within **30 days**.
  2. Via Direct Email Request:
    • Send an email to our dedicated support address: **plateplan@gmail.com**
    • The email subject line **must** be: **"Data Deletion Request"**
    • In the body of the email, please include your **registered Email Address** and/or your **Facebook User ID** so we can locate your account.
    • What Happens Next: We will process the deletion of your account and associated personal data within **30 days** of confirming your request.

Data Retention after Deletion Request

Once the deletion request is confirmed through either method:

  • All personal data collected via Facebook Login will be **permanently deleted** from our live production systems.
  • We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you for internal analytics and service improvement.
  • A small amount of data may be retained in secure backup systems for a limited period (e.g., 90 days) but is subject to deletion upon the next scheduled backup cleanup and is not accessible by our live systems.
Important Note: The deletion process only covers the data Plate Plan has collected. If you wish to delete your entire Facebook account, you must do so directly through Facebook's settings.
